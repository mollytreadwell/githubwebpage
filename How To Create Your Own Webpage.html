How To Create Your Own Webpage Using Github

  • First, you will need to set up your new Github account by providing your email address and establishing a username and password.
  • Next, click the "Create a new repository" button
  • Here you will establish a name for your repo: use something relavant to your website followed by .github.io. One example could be "mollyswebsite.github.io"
  • You will be directed to a page where you can create a new file or upload an existing one. Click the link "uploading an existing file"
  • Now you will drag your files into the box as in indicated, or upload manually.
  • Once you have added your files, click "Commit changes" at the bottom of the page.
  • Nice work! You have published your own webpage!
